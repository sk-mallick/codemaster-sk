Refund Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Deposit Payments

The initial 50% deposit (or agreed booking fee) is non-refundable once work has officially commenced, as it allocates design and development resources to your project.

If a project is cancelled mid-way, clients will be invoiced for hours worked or milestones completed up to the date of cancellation. Any work completed will be handed over upon payment of the balance.
